Baahubali Movie Review

Movie Overview

Title: Baahubali: The Beginning

Director: S.S. Rajamouli

Release Date: July 10, 2015

Genre: Action, Drama

Duration: 159 minutes

Plot Summary

Shivudu discovers his royal heritage and sets out to rescue his kidnapped mother from a tyrant, with grand visuals and strong performances.

Review

Rating: 4.5/5

Baahubali is a visual spectacle with exceptional VFX, grand set pieces, and powerful performances, though its length might be overwhelming for some.

Highlights

  • Visual Effects: Impressive VFX and grand set designs.
  • Action Sequences: Well-choreographed and intense.
  • Performances: Strong lead performances.
  • Music: Complementary and emotional soundtrack.
  • Costume and Design: Detailed and authentic.

Drawbacks

  • Length: Lengthy runtime affects pacing.
  • Predictable Plot: Some predictable elements.
  • Character Development: Secondary characters lack depth.
  • Dialogue: Occasionally melodramatic.

Final Thoughts

An epic blend of action, drama, and mythology, making it a landmark film in Indian cinema. A must-watch for fans of grand stories and stunning visuals.
